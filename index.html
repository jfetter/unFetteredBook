About Jillian

Web Developer by Day, Writer by Night, Awesome, Always.

  • 1983-2001

    Childhood

    I put the Fetter in Tree Fort while playing with my big brother in the treehouse our dad built us in a walnut tree behind our house in Northridge, CA. Then we moved to Thousand Oaks, CA where I did typical suburban things until the end of HS

  • 2001-2005

    In Which I get an Environmental Studies Degree

    This was probably the height of my awkwardness and socialphobia. Side note: I left for college my dad's "rib eatin' buddy" and I came home a meek vegetarian. #loveMyAnimalFriends

  • 2005-2013

    Life In The Bay Area

    I learned the true meaning of "delicious" when I first ate at Mandalay Burmese Restaurant. Worked at the best moving company in the Bay Area. Bought A House. Got Engaged. Got un-engaged and then started to live unfettered.

  • 2014-present

    Jillian2.0

    I'm loving life in Phoenix AZ. I've taken my destiny into my own hands and I'm getting after a career as a web developer. I aunt the fuck out of it with my nieces a couple times a week and I use my brain, make people laugh and enjoy the fuck out of my life every day!

UnFettered puts you behind the eyes of a late bloomer with an active imagination, a craving for romance, and a flawed sense of how to appropriately express herself. After twenty-nine years of playing it safe, Jillian steps away from the comfort of her functional life in search of something more. Her quest to define and obtain fulfillment lead her to explore new living situations, dip a toe in the Internet dating scene, battle blood-sucking insects and confront life’s irksome questions: What is a meaningful existence? When is the right time to walk away from love? are cold sores really that bad?
